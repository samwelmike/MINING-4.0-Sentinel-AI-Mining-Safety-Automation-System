## 📌 Project Overview
**SHK Predictive Maintenance & Workers Safety Automation System** is a real-time mining safety monitoring and automation system designed to minimize workplace accidents and prevent unexpected machine breakdowns. By integrating real-time data processing with automated alert dispatching, the system ensures a proactive approach to safety management in the mining sector.

## 🚀 Key Modules & Functionalities

### 1. Main Dashboard (Operational Overview)
Provides a bird's-eye view of the mine's health, including:
* **Fleet Health Index:** Monitoring the status of all mobile equipment.
* **Environmental Safety:** Real-time atmospheric conditions.
* **Overall Mine Risk:** A live indicator of the current safety status (Low, Moderate, or Critical).

### 2. The Risk Engine (Predictive Maintenance)
Monitors critical machines including **Dump Trucks, Drilling Rigs, Excavators, Loaders, and Slurry Pumps**.
* **Automated Temperature Tracking:** If a machine (e.g., Excavator ZX173) exceeds its safe thermal threshold, the system triggers an **instant SMS alert** to the maintenance team to prevent engine failure.

### 3. Environmental Monitor
Tracks hazardous conditions across different zones:
* **Oxygen Levels:** (e.g., monitoring Tunnel B for hypoxia risks).
* **Gas Detection:** Tracking Methane levels in Open Pits and Dust levels in Processing Plants.
* **Noise Monitoring:** Ensuring compliance with safety standards in the Crushing Plant.

### 4. Personnel Safety Feed (Fatigue Management)
Focuses on the "Human Factor" of mining safety:
* **Biometric Monitoring:** Tracks worker fatigue levels.
* **Smart Decision Support:** Alerts management when an employee (e.g., Digital Application Lead) reaches critical fatigue, suggesting shift rotations to prevent human-error accidents.

### 5. Central Brain & Live Alerts
* **Central Brain:** An intelligent analysis hub that calculates a **Global Risk Score** based on all incoming data points.
* **Live Alerts:** A summarized feed of every machine, zone, or person currently at risk for immediate emergency response.
